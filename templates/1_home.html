About

Welcome to our library management system!

Our website allows librarians to manage a collection of books and customers. Here are some of the features:

  • Add and view books: You can add new books to the collection, including their title, author, publication year, and type.

  • Add and view customers: You can add new customers to the library, including their name, city, and age.

  • Borrow and return books: Customers can borrow books from the library and return them within the specified loan duration.

  • Search: You can search for customers by name or books by title to retrieve relevant information.

  • View loans: You can see a list of all the current loans, including the customer's name, book's title, loan date, and return date.

  • View late loans: You can check for any loans that have not been returned on time.

    • Our goal is to provide an efficient and user-friendly platform for librarians to manage their library operations. We hope you find our system helpful!
